About Me

I am a 4th-year PhD student at the Center for Autonomous Systems and Technologies (CAST) at Caltech. My research focuses on aerial robotics, developing control methods for multi-modal robots capable of transitioning between air and ground locomotion.

Research

Momentum Theory

Investigating control volume placement and conservation equations to optimize the actuator disk model for propulsion systems.

Aerial Robotics

Designing and testing multi-modal robots, such as the **Aerially Transforming Morphobot (ATMO)**, capable of dynamically switching between aerial and terrestrial modes.
